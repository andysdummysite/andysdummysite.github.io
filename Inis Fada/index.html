Our Philosphy

The band was originally formed with the intention of being a competition band. As such, we have always worked towards the highest degree of excellence possible. To this day that is still our primary objective, but we never lose sight of the importance of family and fun. While we work very hard toward doing our best in competition, we make sure that the families of band members are included in our activities. When we travel to competitions we encourage our members to bring their families along so everyone can enjoy the festivities of the day. Of course when it is time to compete that is the sole focus for that period of time.
